# ✈️ Airline Passenger Satisfaction Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes airline passenger satisfaction data using Power BI to identify the key factors influencing customer satisfaction. The dashboard provides insights into passenger demographics, travel characteristics, service quality, and satisfaction levels, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions to improve the overall customer experience.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze passenger satisfaction across different customer segments.
- Identify the factors that influence customer satisfaction.
- Evaluate airline service quality and travel experience.
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard for business users.
- Generate actionable business insights and recommendations.

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Business Class passengers report higher satisfaction levels than Economy Class passengers.
- Inflight Entertainment and Online Boarding are among the highest-rated services.
- Flight delays negatively impact passenger satisfaction.
- Loyal customers tend to have higher satisfaction compared to first-time customers.
- Service quality plays a significant role in overall passenger satisfaction.

---

## 📌 Business Recommendations

- Improve Wi-Fi connectivity and onboard services for Economy Class passengers.
- Reduce departure and arrival delays to improve customer experience.
- Focus on improving lower-rated services through customer feedback.
- Strengthen loyalty programs to encourage repeat travel.
- Continuously monitor passenger feedback to enhance service quality.

---

## 📈 Project Outcome

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of passenger satisfaction by analyzing customer demographics, travel patterns, and service quality. It enables stakeholders to identify improvement areas, monitor performance, and support data-driven decisions to enhance the passenger experience.
